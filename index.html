About Us

Maharashtra Career Academy, located at Karamala Tembhurni Road, Kumbhej Fata, Jeur, Maharashtra, is dedicated to fostering excellence in competitive exam preparation for careers in the police, army, and intelligence sectors.

Operating under the esteemed Chatrapati Shivaji Shikshan Sanstha, our academy emphasizes discipline, focus, and determination—qualities essential for success in defense and government service examinations.

Our unique outdoor classroom sessions provide students with a spacious, natural environment that promotes concentration and well-being while maintaining social distancing. This innovative approach bridges educational gaps and encourages a healthy balance between study and physical health.

We cultivate a strong sense of community and team spirit through group activities and celebrations, enhancing morale and camaraderie among students.
